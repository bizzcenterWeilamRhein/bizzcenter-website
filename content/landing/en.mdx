<FAQ title="Frequently asked questions">
  <FAQItem question="Can I try a day for free?">
    You can book a day pass — e.g. for coworking or a day office. We'll gladly credit it toward a monthly package or a 10-day pass.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="What is the minimum term?">
    There are no long commitments. Coworking from 1 day, offices from 1 month, virtual office from 1 month. Monthly cancellable.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="What is included in the price?">
    WiFi, kitchen, printer, reception, mail service and access to community events. No hidden costs.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Is parking available?">
    Yes, customer parking spaces are available for your first visit — but not for permanent use. Parking spaces can be optionally added. Bicycle parking is available directly at the Kesselhaus.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Where is bizzcenter Weil am Rhein located?">
    In the historic Kesselhaus, Am Kesselhaus 3, 79576 Weil am Rhein — just 300 meters from the Swiss border. Reachable via tram line 8 from Basel (stop Riedlistrasse/Kesselhaus). EuroAirport Basel-Mulhouse is 15 minutes away by car.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Can I use bizzcenter from Basel or Lörrach?">
    Yes, many of our customers come from Basel, Lörrach and Rheinfelden. Thanks to its location right on the Swiss border and the tram connection, bizzcenter is quickly accessible — ideal as a coworking space near Basel or office in Weil am Rhein.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="What is a business address and why do I need one?">
    A business address is a legally valid address you can use for trade registration, commercial register, imprint and tax office — without having to rent your own office. Ideal for freelancers, startups and companies that want to appear professional.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Is the business address suitable for Swiss companies?">
    Yes. Especially for Swiss companies that need a German business address near Basel — e.g. for the German market, commercial register or as a billing address. We also offer a Swiss phone number with forwarding.
  </FAQItem>
</FAQ>
