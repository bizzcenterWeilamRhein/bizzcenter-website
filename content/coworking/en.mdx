<FAQ title="Frequently asked questions about coworking">
  <FAQItem question="In which rooms does coworking take place?">
    The two main rooms are on the ground floor of the tower building right by the main entrance — this is also where the two Quiet Boxes for phone calls and focused work are located. There is also an additional coworking office in the Green Office.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Who is coworking suitable for?">
    For self-employed people, startups, freelancers, small teams and employees who want to work flexibly — without long-term office rental contracts.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Who is coworking less suitable for?">
    Coworking is less suitable for activities with high public traffic, high noise levels or the need for permanently exclusive, enclosed offices. For such cases, we offer single offices that can also be rented by the day or long-term.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Can I try coworking first?">
    Yes. Book a day pass first — it will be credited if you make a longer-term booking.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Is there coworking without a minimum term?">
    Yes. Coworking is flexibly available by the day (10-day pass) or monthly. With longer notice periods you benefit from cheaper prices.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="What services are included in the coworking price?">
    A fully equipped workspace in a quiet atmosphere, fast WiFi, utilities, use of the common areas as well as kitchen, restroom and chillout area.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Are there fixed workspaces or only flexible seats?">
    Both. Fixed workspaces (fix desks) can be booked — so your monitor, for example, stays in place. Day passes and 10-day passes use the flex desks.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="How quiet is the coworking?">
    Our coworking offices are designed for a maximum of 3–4 workstations. This ensures quiet, productive work — no noisy open-plan coworking. For those who prefer even more quiet, a single office can be booked.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="How fast is the internet?">
    High-speed internet with WIFI 5/6 and LAN. Stable enough for video conferencing, cloud applications and IP telephony.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Are there meeting or conference rooms?">
    Yes. Two Quiet Cubes are available free of charge for confidential conversations. In addition, meeting and conference rooms can be booked flexibly by the hour or day.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Can I use the address as a business address?">
    Yes. With the appropriate plans, the address can be used as a business address and is valid for imprint purposes.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Is there mail acceptance or mail forwarding?">
    Yes. Depending on the package, parcel acceptance is possible, optionally also mail forwarding — especially in combination with virtual office services. Parcels can be picked up 24/7 with your own door code.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Is there 24/7 access?">
    Yes. All coworking users receive an individual access code — 24/7, seven days a week, independent of opening hours.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Is there good transport access and parking?">
    Yes. Tram line 8 (stop Riedlistrasse/Kesselhaus), close to the A5 motorway, parking on site with 90 minutes free parking as well as optional day and monthly tickets.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="What is the difference between coworking and a traditional office?">
    Coworking offers an open, flexible working environment with networking opportunities. A single office offers more privacy and exclusivity for focused work.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="What common areas are part of the coworking?">
    In addition to the workstations, there is a modern, plant-filled chillout area as well as a fully equipped kitchen with fridge, cooking facilities and seating.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Are monitors available?">
    Yes. 27-inch curved monitors can be optionally added — for ergonomic and productive working.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Are there lockable cabinets?">
    Yes. Lockable lockers and cabinets in various sizes are optionally available to store work materials and valuables securely.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="What cleaning products are used?">
    We rely on ecological cleaning products from Sonett and Frosch — because sustainability is important to us down to the smallest detail.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Is there lunch nearby?">
    Yes — there's a restaurant with daily lunch menu right in the Kesselhaus. Perfect for lunch breaks without having to leave the building.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Can I bring my child?">
    Yes. In the Green Office there's a cozy play box where you can play together with your child or where older children can also keep themselves busy for a while. Please note that this is not a childcare service — supervision remains with the parents. So all coworkers can work in focus, we ask that you don't use the play box as a permanent unsupervised solution.
  </FAQItem>
</FAQ>
