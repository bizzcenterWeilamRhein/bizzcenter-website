In collaboration with the Economic Development Office of the City of Weil am Rhein, the innovative Green Office concept was created. In the heritage-listed halls of the former Schwarzenbach silk weaving mill, a unique concept for coworkers, founders and small businesses has been developed — an inspiring place for sustainable working and creative development.

The impressive ceiling height of 7.50 meters gives the hall a spacious, open feel and ensures a pleasant working atmosphere in the heritage-listed Kesselhaus Weil am Rhein. The ideal location right in the border triangle of Germany, France and Switzerland offers our customers a wide range of advantages.

At only 300m from Basel, the Kesselhaus is accessible cross-border directly via Basel tram line 8 (stop Riedlistr./Kesselhaus).

Our stylish offices provide a warm, quiet environment, while generous glass elements ensure plenty of daylight and an airy feel. A well-designed greening concept with potted and climbing plants creates a healthy indoor climate and promotes productive work.

Welcome to a new era of sustainable working!
