<FAQ title="Frequently asked questions about conference rooms">
  <FAQItem question="How can I book a conference room?">
    Simply send an inquiry by phone, email or via our contact form. We will confirm availability at short notice.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Can I book the room by the hour?">
    Yes. All rooms can be booked by the hour, half day or full day — whatever suits your needs.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Is catering available?">
    Yes. From a simple coffee break to a full lunch, we're happy to arrange your meeting catering.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="What technology is available?">
    Projector, screen, whiteboard and high-speed internet (WIFI 5/6 + LAN) are available in all rooms.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Is there parking?">
    Yes. Parking spaces are located directly in front of the building and can be optionally booked at a daily rate of EUR 6,- excl. VAT. First 90 minutes free.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="How do I get to bizzcenter by public transport?">
    Tram line 8 from Basel, stop Riedlistrasse/Kesselhaus — right at our doorstep.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Can I view the room beforehand?">
    Yes. Arrange a no-obligation viewing — we'll gladly show you our premises.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Is food available on site?">
    Yes — there's a restaurant with daily lunch menu right in the Kesselhaus. Perfect for longer meetings or full-day bookings.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Where exactly is the conference room located in Weil am Rhein?">
    In the historic Kesselhaus, Am Kesselhaus 3, 79576 Weil am Rhein — just 300 meters from the Swiss border. Directly accessible via tram line 8 from Basel (stop Riedlistrasse/Kesselhaus) and close to the A5 motorway.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Can I book a conference room as a Swiss company?">
    Yes. Many of our customers come from Basel and northwestern Switzerland. The border is within walking distance, and prices are in euros — significantly cheaper than comparable rooms in Basel.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="How far is the conference room from EuroAirport Basel?">
    EuroAirport Basel-Mulhouse-Freiburg is only about 15 minutes away by car. Ideal for meetings with international participants.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Are the rooms suitable for workshops and training?">
    Yes. Rooms L (15 people) and XL (25 people) are especially well-suited for full-day workshops, training sessions and seminars. A setup fee for alternative seating arrangements can be booked optionally.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Can I also book the conference room on weekends?">
    Yes. Our rooms are available 7 days a week — including Saturdays and Sundays.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="What does a conference room in Weil am Rhein cost?">
    From EUR 19,- per hour for Meeting Room S (2 people). Half-day and full-day rates are available. With the 10-day pass you save 15% on all bookings.
  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Is there a conference room with daylight?">
    Yes — all our rooms are daylight-flooded. The Kesselhaus has 7.50 meter ceilings and large window fronts.
  </FAQItem>
</FAQ>
